About

"I design experiences that help people move forward with confidence in high-stakes, complex environments — whether that's a factory operator managing security assets, or a first-time user completing a financial verification flow."

I'm a UX designer with 6 years at TXOne Networks, a B2B OT cybersecurity company, where I lead design across two workflows: PM-initiated product development and weekly VIP client feature sessions. In both, my role is the same — understand the real problem, propose design solutions, and navigate the space between what clients want, what engineering can build, and what the global product roadmap allows.

My background spans industrial design (NCKU), graduate-level UX research, and hands-on work designing hardware+software integrated systems. I've conducted contextual field research at factory sites, run mixed-method studies, and built Design Systems from the ground up.

I'm drawn to products where the stakes are high and the constraints are real — which is why I'm moving toward fintech, where good design genuinely changes whether someone trusts a platform with their money.
